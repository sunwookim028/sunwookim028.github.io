Reference - FIG. 11 (RTL-verified depths)

  • fp_add LATENCY=2: {align+add} | {normalize LZC+shift+exp} | {pack}. 2 register FFs (p1, p2), result 2 clocks after inputs. LUT + CARRY8; the normalize half was the 200 MHz worst cone. Source: fpu/fp_add.sv (lat2 generate arm).
  • fp_mul LATENCY=2: {driver+unpack} | {DSP48 multiply} | {round+pack}. 2 register FFs (g_reg_unpack at L>=2, g_reg_product at L>=1). Vivado infers one DSP48E2 for the 24x24 mantissa multiply. Source: fpu/fp_mul.sv.
  • Why they are the atoms: the PE MAC, the mxu_accumulator RMW, every VPU function (gelu / exp2 / reduce / SIMD), and the exp2 BRAM x - floor(x) subtract all instantiate these two. Their 2-cycle depth is what sets PE_LATENCY, D_vpu, and the reduce-tree latency downstream.
