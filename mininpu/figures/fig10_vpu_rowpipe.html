one tile-row in the VPU pipeline (D_vpu = 23 deep) pipeline-drained (all rows done)

D_vpu is the per-row drain latency of the fixed-depth VPU pipeline, not a serial per-row cost. The latency-serial schedule (top) pays it 8 times (8 x 23 = 184 cy) and leaves the 23 stages mostly empty. The row-pipelined schedule (bottom) issues one tile-row per cycle, so all 8 rows are in flight across the pipeline at once; the whole tile drains in ~8 + 23 = 31 cy (~1 row/cy). This is why D_vpu is negligible for performance: it is paid once per tile and hidden behind the following rows, amortized across the tile-loop.
