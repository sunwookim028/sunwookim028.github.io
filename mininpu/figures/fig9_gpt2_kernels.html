910MLP fc1 output-tile window — 783 cyc (K = Dm/8 = 96)

systolic fill then 96 K-tiles of matmul; acc.store + gelu of the prior tile collapse into the first ~28 cyc, VPU idle for the rest

920Attention block — 408 cyc SERIAL (decision B: no overlap)

four non-overlapping phases across MXU/VPU lanes; softmax dependency stalls the block; the softmax‖matmul overlap is deliberately forfeited

930LayerNorm — Dm=768 = 96 D-tiles (two passes)

Pass-1 packs 3 ops into a zero-idle 24-cyc slot; Pass-2 is single-port-SPAD bandwidth-bound (32 accesses/tile > 24 VPU cyc)

935devmem C = A @ B (16×16 bf16) — DRAM-bandwidth-bound

isolated devmem→devmem matmul; A+B load (1024 B) and C store (512 B) over the 4 B/cy DRAM HP port dwarf the 160-cy 8-matmul compute (MXU busy just 79 cy) · DMU single-outstanding serializes A,B

Roofline opposite of the MLP panels: a tiny isolated devmem matmul is memory-bound, not MXU-bound — the on-chip DM bus (32 B/cy) sits ~8× idle behind the 4 B/cy DRAM HP port (MXU busy only 79 of 544 cy). This is why decode wants wider DRAM/HBM [v3]. Load/store also carry a one-time DRAM access latency on top, dwarfed by the BW term. Tile-granular prefetch could hide the 160-cy compute under the 256-cy load (total → ~load+store = 384 cy), still DRAM-BW-bound.
